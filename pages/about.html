About Us

After the boom of Web1 and Web2(Old and
Present Internet), where there were just static pages and then interaction
between users and the platforms, we are finally adapting to Web3.
Web3 is the latest version of the internet,
but many people don’t know much about it. Hence we introduce Edu3.
Edu3 aims to teach people the basics of
Web3 with the help of short chapters and quizzes.
*This project is a submission of ‘Hack Web3conf’
  Hackathon for the event Web3conf India.
